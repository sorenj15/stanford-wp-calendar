Bernardo Herzer

Goalkeeper — USC

The Goalkeeper

Bernardo Herzer arrives at Stanford as one of the most decorated goalkeepers in college water polo. The 6’6” Los Angeles native appeared in 58 games over three seasons at USC, amassing 480 career saves. A two-time All-American, he earned ACWPC First Team honors in 2024 after averaging 9.3 saves per game across 25 appearances, also earning NCAA and MPSF All-Tournament Team recognition. In 2023, he earned All-America Honorable Mention distinction averaging 9.6 saves per game, finishing with double-digit saves in nine games, including 10 in both the MPSF final and NCAA semifinal.

USA Senior National Team

Herzer has competed for the USA Men’s Senior National Team, appearing at the 2025 World Aquatics Championships in Singapore. He developed his game internationally at the Barcelona International Water Polo Academy, training alongside some of Europe’s top talent. Originally from Los Angeles, he attended Mira Costa High School where he was named Bay League MVP as a senior.

James Rozolis-Hill

Utility — Harvard

Harvard’s All-Time Leading Scorer

James Rozolis-Hill is one of the most prolific scorers in the history of college water polo. The Huntington Beach native set Harvard’s career goals record with 358 scores across three seasons with the Crimson. He became the first player in program history to score over 100 goals in a season, doing it as a freshman with 123, then repeated the feat as a sophomore (109) and broke his own single-season record as a junior (124). A three-time ACWPC All-American, he is the second All-American transfer to join Stanford in as many weeks, following Bernardo Herzer.

The 2-Meter Threat

Rozolis-Hill is a genuine nightmare at the center position. His combination of elite finishing from center, creativity, and dynamic play at the 2-meter position make him one of the most dangerous centers in the country. He has been on the Cutino Award Watch List, given annually to the best player in college water polo, in each of his three collegiate seasons, and has competed in USA Water Polo Senior National Team tournaments.
